What is Wild Swimming? Wild Swimming is swimming in open water in the great outdoors

What are the benefits of Wild Swimming?

Having New Experiences

  • Experience Nature in a new way
  • Make new Friends allong the way
  • Swim organised races or on spontaneous dips
  • Challenge your self
  • And have loads of Fun

Enjoy Health Benefits

  • Weight loss
  • Minimise Pain
  • Immune System Boost
  • Personal Achievement
  • Increasing Your Energy

What you need to start Wild Swimming?

Checklist to start swimming

A safe place to swim

A brightly coloured swimming hat

A Wet suit and boots

Friends to Swim with
